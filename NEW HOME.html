Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Generally, people between the ages of 18 and 65 who are in good health and weigh at least 50kg can donate blood.

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood once every 56 days. Other types of blood donations may vary.

What happens to my blood after donation?

Your donated blood is tested, processed, and separated into components for patients in need.
